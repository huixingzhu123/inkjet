Our Company

  What we do?

De Color Technology Co., Ltd is specialized in re-manufacturing inkjet cartridges with printheads and the drum cartridges for copiers. With every step of the production strictly controlled and in accordance with international process standard,and the state-of-the-art facilities, we pride ourselves in providing quality remanfactured cartridges.

 Why choose us?

Stringent quality control assures page yield and quality comparable to OEMs. We have the state-of-the-art facility to remanufacture the Cartridges. We use high quality raw material and components. Each cartridge is tested before being shipped.
